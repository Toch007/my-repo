Kelechi Nnam

Kelechi Nnam is a trained Engineer and a practicing Business Development Expert with a flair for good music.

A counter Tenor of high repute, songwriter and worship leader with interests in music composition and choral conducting.

He joined the Laz Ekwueme Chorale in 2005 where he got most of his training from HRH Igwe Prof Laz Ekwueme.

He holds a diploma in music and is currently the music Director of The Chorus Ensemble.

He has served as Choirmaster and Music Director in various Anglican and Methodist Churches in Nigeria.

He is currently the Deputy Choirmaster of St. Matthew's Anglican Church Maitama, and is married to Chidera.

Lawal Omeiza Godwin

Lawal Omeiza Godwin is a pianist who has performed several piano solos and choral works by Nigerian and Western composers from different musical eras. He is also a fine organist.
He is a graduate of Building.

His passion for new skills and acquiring knowledge led to his discovery and love for music.

He has a Diploma in Church Music Performance and has gained a lot of experience serving as a Pianist/Organist in various capacities in different parts of Nigeria.

He served as an organist and director of music at the St. John's Anglican Church, Ohueta, Diocese of Okene, an Organist at the Independent Baptist Church, Tunga, Minna, Niger state, as a visiting organist at the Anglican Church of the Pentecost, Mabushi, and St Matthew's Anglican Church Maitama, both located in Abuja.

He is also the assistant accompanist/pianist for the prestigious Minna Choral society, was the resident Pianist for the Rising star recital series project in 2021 for the Nigerian philharmonic company, Abuja, is currently a member of the Festival Of Praise Choir And Orchestra (FOPCAO), Abuja, Nigeria, and one of the accompanists of The Chorus Ensemble.
