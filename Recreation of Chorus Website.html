About Us

Who is The Chorus?

The Chorus is

A non-denominational, multi-talented group of musicians, both vocalists and instrumentalist, in the city of Abuja, Nigeria who have come together to raise the bar as long as music is concerned. The chorus is set to take music to a whole new dimension in Nigeria and to partner with other music chorals at home and abroad improve music as seen in these parts of the world.
